Dave is a research software engineer at Newcastle University, who has recently returned to academia from industry. His professional interest and development work is currently in the development of web applications that allow the analysis and visualisation of large datasets, specifically high throughput genome sequencing data. This evolving and emerging scientific landscape introduces new and exciting software engineering challenges.  

He is passionate about mental health and wellbeing in the workplace, and works with several projects to raise awareness and reduce the stigma associated with talking about mental health.  In DisCouRSE he is leading the further development of the [Digital Research Competencies Framework](https://direct-framework.github.io/digital-research-competencies-framework/).

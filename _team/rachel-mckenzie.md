Rachel is a Professional Research Investment & Strategy Manager (PRISM) at UCL’s [Advanced Research Computing Centre](https://www.ucl.ac.uk/arc), where she is responsible for research communication and community building.
She plays a pivotal role in supporting community development and communicating research effectively with a broad and diverse range of stakeholders, both internally and externally.
In DisCouRSE, Rachel will lead the project’s communications and support the development, visibility, and long-term sustainability of the project’s community-building activities, helping to connect and strengthen communities across the dRTP landscape.

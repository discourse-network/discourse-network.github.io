# Funded Projects

The DisCouRSE [Flexible Fund](/funding/round-1) supports community-led projects aligned with our goals,
enabling aspiring leaders to trial approaches to enhancing skills and career opportunities within their local contexts,
build new connections through joint initiatives, and strategically assess options for future investment and activity.
All projects that have been awarded funding so far are listed here, and updates will be added as they progress.

**Peer Ladder is a peer-mentoring initiative that empowers underrepresented digital Research Technical
Professionals (dRTPs)** to climb the leadership ladder together – building confidence, community and inclusive
leadership across UK research-performing organisations (RPOs).

dRTPs are essential to data-driven research, yet many remain in supporting roles without clear routes into
leadership. Women, disabled people, LGBTQIA+, and minority ethnic professionals are particularly under-
represented at senior levels, limiting diversity of thought and innovation
([BSA briefing](https://www.britishscienceassociation.org/news/briefing-on-equality-diversity-and-inclusion-strategies-in-stem-makes-business-case-for-growth),
[House of Commons report](https://publications.parliament.uk/pa/cm5803/cmselect/cmsctech/95/summary.html)).

**Peer Ladder** builds on a **proven European model ([ELEAD](https://elixir-europe.org/how-we-work/scientific-programme/people/elead2))**
that successfully enabled women leaders within the European ELIXIR life-science infrastructure.
The UK pilot adapts this approach to all underrepresented dRTP groups,
focusing on **peer mentoring** – the most impactful element of the original model. Participants receive structured
peer support and **training from Open Life Science (OLS)**, which provides onboarding and facilitation to create a
safe, inclusive learning environment that builds allyship and leadership skills.

As a test-pilot, Peer Ladder will **demonstrate the value of this model nationally**. Participants will be supported
with materials and guidance to continue meeting as a self-organised group beyond the project.

Outcomes will feed into DisCouRSE, ELIXIR-UK, and SSI to shape inclusive leadership and EDI policy across the digital
research community. Visit the [project website](https://peerladder.elixiruknode.org/) to find out more information.

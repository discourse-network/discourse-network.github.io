**Many dRTPs** already demonstrate strong leadership in their daily work, supporting research projects, teams, and the wider research environment. However, their contributions are often overlooked or undervalued.

**This project aims to highlight and build on dRTPs existing strengths**. Through a participatory approach this project will explore how dRTPs lead, collaborate, and influence research, while identifying ways institutions can better recognise and support their work.

**By linking these insights to the development of locally launched** **Leadership Programmes** to run alongside existing academic development initiatives, the project ensures that technical research professionals receive development opportunities comparable to those available to researchers in traditional academic roles. 

**This project will inform new career pathways to embed the recognition of technical expertise**, leadership, and innovation into formal progression structures.
The ultimate goal of this project is to demonstrate how to facilitate a research culture that is more inclusive and sustainable, to step into a direction where the leadership, expertise, and contributions of technical research professionals are acknowledged, nurtured, and leveraged to support high-quality research.

DisCouRSE lead Jonathan Cooper will take part in a panel on "People, Practices, and Performance: Building a Responsible HPC-AI Culture".
He will present on how our work to develop communities of leaders is particularly relevant for the HPC and AI fields,
and advertise the [flexible funding call](/funding/round-1).
He will also summarise the career pathways at UCL's [Advanced Research Computing Centre](https://www.ucl.ac.uk/arc) as an example of what can be achieved.
